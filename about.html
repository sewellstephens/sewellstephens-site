Meet the owner

Hi, I'm Sewell Stephens. Founder and owner of Sewell Stephens software and 16 years old. I build software that clicks. Sewellstephens.com has a long rather interesting history. Before I built Sewellstephens.com, I had some experience in web design but I was terrible at designing uis back then. I watched an Html tutorial in my Dads friends living room, and wrote some basic html. Before that I did play a bit around with the inspector panel in the browser, but nothing serious yet. After that I kind of lost interest in coding for a year in a half, although I did have a messy Wix website that looked trashy. It had tons of elements piled on top each other and I decided that green, purple, and a bit of yellow were good colors. Anyway in december 2017 I decided I had enough of the free wix site and at that point they had released Wix Velo. It was called Wix Code at the time later renamed Wix Corvid being changed again due to Covid-19. Anyway, Wix Velo is a database system and coding system designed by Wix that is not really talked about much in the Web development world. Its pretty straight forward ounce you get the hang of it. Anyway, I used it in an attempt to build an advertising software under the name sewellstephens.com later adding a web development option. I then 3 years of attempting to build the software shut it down. Then I built a GDPR compliance software named Rototer in December 2020. I ended up finishing it later shuting down also on August 2021. Fast forward three months I started Twayobiz in May of the same year. I still have it running to this day. Then I moved to a screen recording software Monkeycord that I never built. Now I am planning another product Sheaterbob. You can view all my products here. I hope you have a good rest of your day and bye.

 

Sewell Stephens, Single Founder and Owner
